Learn More

Rise and Shine Pottery invites you to a creative oasis where pottery crafting meets a warm, inviting atmosphere. Catering to art enthusiasts and those seeking a therapeutic escape, the studio features an array of pottery creations, each telling a story of individual flair.
Step into the world of Rise and Shine Pottery, where spinning wheels and the scent of clay set the stage for an immersive experience. The studio offers workshops for all skill levels, allowing visitors to shape clay into personalized masterpieces.
Beyond the potter's wheel, Rise and Shine Pottery hosts exhibitions featuring local artists' works, adding to the studio's vibrant ambiance.
Rejuvenate at our cozy cafe with freshly brewed coffee and artisanal pastries. Whether it's a solitary moment or a gathering with fellow art enthusiasts, Rise and Shine Pottery provides a charming setting.
Rise and Shine Pottery invites you to awaken your creativity. Join us in a space where artistic passion meets the pleasure of creating something beautiful, leaving you with lasting memories.

Rates and Packages

POTTERY EXPERIENCE 1800Php/ PAX(min 2 pax to sign up)

Create your own masterpiece. Single session 1-1.5 hr. 1 finished work. Combination of wheel throwing and handbuilding class. Glazing and firing services are already included. Output: Mug, personalised pot, bowl, decors.

BASIC WHEEL THROWING OR HANDBUILDING WORKSHOP 2500 per pax

Single session is 3 hours. You will be able to make 3 pots/cups of your own design. Your works will also be something you can use later on as food vessels or high quality ceramic decor arts. Glazing and firing services are already included.

GROUP SPECIAL WORKSHOP (6 pax and above) 2500 per pax

Workshop will already be a combination of wheel throwing and handbuilding (already at 50% discount @2500 per pax, instead of 5k). 3 hours class. You will be able to make 4 outputs of your own design (2 on wheel throwing, 2 on handbuilding). Your works will also be something you can use later on as food vessels or high quality ceramic decor arts. Glazing and firing services are already included.
