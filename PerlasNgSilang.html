Perlas ng Silang Flower Park and Restaurant is a captivating oasis nestled in the heart of Silang, Cavite. This enchanting destination offers a harmonious blend of natural beauty and delectable cuisine, making it a must-visit for flower enthusiasts and food connoisseurs alike. The park boasts a lush and vibrant collection of flowers that create a picturesque backdrop for visitors, providing the perfect setting for leisurely strolls and moments of tranquility. As you explore the grounds, you'll encounter a diverse array of blooms, each adding its unique charm to the landscape. The restaurant within Perlas ng Silang complements the natural beauty with an array of delectable Asian-inspired dishes. From savory culinary delights to sweet treats, the menu is designed to satisfy every palate. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner under the stars or a family brunch, the restaurant offers a delightful dining experience. Perlas ng Silang Flower Park and Restaurant is open daily from 8 am to 8 pm, inviting you to immerse yourself in a world of floral wonder and culinary delight.

Best Sellers

Seafood Laksa

consists of rice noodles, coconut milk, shrimp, bean sprouts, tofu, laksa leaves, and a flavorful broth made with spices such as lemongrass, chili, and curry.

Indonesian Beef Rendang

includes beef simmered in a rich coconut milk-based sauce with a blend of aromatic spices such as lemongrass, galangal, turmeric, and chili.

Pad Thai

includes rice noodles, shrimp or tofu, bean sprouts, peanuts, eggs, and a flavorful sauce made from tamarind paste, fish sauce, and chili.

Crispy Pata

deep-fried pork knuckles or hocks until they become crispy on the outside while remaining tender inside. It's often seasoned with a mix of garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, and served with a dipping sauce.

Fried Salt and Pepper Ribs

consist of pork ribs seasoned with salt, pepper, and sometimes additional spices. The ribs are then deep-fried until crispy, creating a flavorful and crunchy dish.

KungPaoChicken

includes diced chicken stir-fried with peanuts, vegetables (such as bell peppers and zucchini), and chili peppers.

Crispy Kare Kare

crispy pork belly or oxtail served with a rich and savory Filipino Kare-Kare sauce made from ground peanuts, garlic, onions, and annatto. It's often accompanied by vegetables like string beans and eggplant.

Green Papaya Shrimp Salad

includes shredded green papaya, cooked shrimp, cherry tomatoes, lime, fish sauce, peanuts, and Thai chili for a zesty and refreshing salad.

Mango Sticky Rice

includes glutinous rice, ripe mango slices, coconut milk, sugar, and a pinch of salt for a simple and delicious dessert.
